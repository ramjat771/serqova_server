Shipping & Delivery Policy

Effective Date:

1. Overview

Since Serqova provides **on-demand home services** (not physical goods), the term “shipping” in this policy refers to the **delivery and fulfillment of booked services** at the customer’s chosen address.

Our goal is to ensure that each booked service is delivered on time and with complete professionalism by trusted and verified service providers.

2. Service Delivery Process

  • Once a customer books a service, Serqova confirms the booking and assigns a verified service professional.
  • You will receive details such as the assigned professional’s name, contact number, and estimated time of arrival via the app or SMS.
  • The service is delivered at your specified address and within the selected time slot.
  • In rare cases (such as high demand or emergencies), the time slot may be adjusted with prior notice.

3. Service Availability

  • Our services are currently available in select locations across India. Availability depends on your area and the type of service requested.
  • You can check service coverage by entering your address or PIN code in the app before booking.

4. Estimated Delivery Time

  • For on-demand bookings, professionals generally arrive within **60–120 minutes** of confirmation, depending on location and traffic.
  • For scheduled appointments, the service is delivered within the exact time slot chosen by the customer.

5. Delays and Rescheduling

  • In case of delays caused by weather, traffic, or unforeseen conditions, the customer will be notified immediately.
  • Customers can reschedule appointments at no extra cost if done at least **2 hours before the scheduled time**.
  • If a professional fails to reach on time, Serqova will arrange an alternate professional or reschedule at your convenience.

6. Customer Responsibilities

  • Ensure your address and contact details are correct and accessible at the time of service.
  • Be available at the service location to receive the professional and allow entry to the premises.
  • If the customer is unavailable at the scheduled time, the service may be marked as “delivered” and cancellation charges may apply.

7. No Physical Shipping

Serqova does not sell or ship any physical products. All services are performed at the customer’s premises by assigned professionals. Therefore, there are no shipping fees or tracking numbers involved.

8. Contact and Support

If you face any issues related to service delivery, scheduling, or delays, please reach out to our support team immediately.
